# Kireikies Cookies - Implementation Plan

## Project Context

This project is a modern landing page and portfolio for an artisan cookies brand, built with a premium, minimalist, and warm aesthetic.

### Visual Concept

- **Style:** Modern Minimalist with Soft Organic Tones.
- **Palette:** Warm neutrals (cream, beige, soft brown, muted terracotta, warm white).
- **Shapes:** Rounded corners and organic blob shapes.
- **Effects:** Light glassmorphism.
- **Typography:** Elegant pairing of Serif (Headlines) and Sans-serif (Body).
- **Layout:** Spacious with generous white space.

### Page Structure

1. **Sticky Navbar:** Transparent glassmorphism, smooth scrolling, mobile responsive.
2. **Hero Section:** High-impact headline, supporting tagline, CTA, and organic visual composition.
3. **About Brand:** Emotional storytelling with a split layout.
4. **Product Showcase:** Bento-style card grid with elegant hover animations.
5. **Brand Story / Values:** Highlighting craftsmanship and ingredients.
6. **Testimonials:** Soft card reviews in a clean grid/slider.
7. **Contact / Order:** Minimalist form and social links.
8. **Footer:** Brand signature feel with newsletter and social icons.

### Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (v4)
- **Fonts:** Playfair Display (Serif) & Manrope (Sans-serif)
- **Animations:** GSAP & AOS (Scroll Reveal)
- **Icons:** Lucide Icons
- **Enhancements:** Swiper.js (Sliders) & Lenis (Smooth Scrolling)
